Team Train


 For the Team Train project, my team and I were challenged with creating new models of operational trains using Fusion 360 for the Lionel Train Company. Each team member was required to build their own unique train car model (with the theme of aviation), each of which had a different function (e.g., dining, sleeping, storage, etc.), and we had to put each car together to form a whole train assembly.

We decided on the theme of aviation for our project, since many kids are fascinated by flight, and it makes for an engaging activity that they can move around and play with. Being the team idea, engineer, and assembler for this project meant that I was in charge of brainstorming, creating my own car, and putting everyone's cars together into a nice assembly model.
